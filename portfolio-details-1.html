Experience

Project Bonbon is a 3D Turn-based JRPG game currently in development as of Fall 2023 under VGDev.

I am currently programming and producing music and sound effects for the game. My programming job on the team entails utilizing custom tools written for Unity to create new functions and mechanics in the game. In terms of music and sound effects, my job is to create music for the Crystal Cave area of the game that changes dynamically based on environmental and situational variables in the game's battle state machine. In addition to this, I am tasked with creating sound effects using REAPER and various VST effect plugins such as Valhalla Shimmer for the characters weapons.
